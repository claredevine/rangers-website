Creativity Octant


Phase 1: Try out an activity for an hour!

  • Learn to knit
  • Learn how to sew
  • Create a camp blanket
  • Make a bracelet out of loom bands
  • Attend Big Gig
  • Create a scrapbook of The Senior Section Centenary
  • Christmas crafts
  • Make an Easter bonnet
  • Learn morse code
  • Learn a new dance
  • Attend the pantomine or the theatre
  • Hold a charity fashion show
  • Try a paper craft

Phase 2: Develop a skill for 12 hours!

  • Take a course in a language other than your first
  • Take part in a play or orchestra
  • Take lessons in an instrument or craft for a set length of time
  • Make an item of clothing

Phase 3: Further develop a skill for 30 hours!

  • Gain a music grade certificate
  • Complete a dance exam
  • Complete the developing a skill section of the Queen's Guide Award
  • Design and maintain a website on a regular basis
